Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 11, 2026

Short version: Sammy OS collects only what it needs to function. Your files and chat history stay on your machine. API keys are encrypted before storage. No personal data is sold or shared with advertisers. This is a portfolio project — not a commercial product — so please do not store sensitive or irreplaceable information.

1. Who We Are

Sammy OS is a personal portfolio project developed by Braxton Vogel, a software engineering student at Sam Houston State University. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to Braxton Vogel individually.

Contact: braxtonvogel.com

2. What Data We Collect

Usage counts are strictly aggregate — total messages sent, total research reports started, etc. No message content, file names, or personally identifying information is included in telemetry.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • The content of your conversations with Sam
  • The contents or names of files in your Knowledge Vault
  • Screenshots or screen recordings taken by the screen-share feature
  • Audio transcripts from the transcription feature
  • Your IP address (beyond what Vercel's infrastructure logs automatically)
  • Browsing history or behavior outside the App

All AI conversations and file data are processed locally on your machine and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party AI Providers

When you send a message to Sam, your message is sent to a third-party AI provider (Groq, Google Gemini, Cerebras, OpenAI, Anthropic, or others depending on your settings). These requests are made directly from the app using your API keys. Each provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your prompts:

  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy

We do not control how these providers handle data sent to them. Review their policies before using their services.

5. Data Storage and Security

Account data (email, hashed password, encrypted API keys) is stored in Upstash Redis, a managed cloud database. Upstash operates under its own privacy policy and maintains SOC 2 compliance.

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including password hashing with bcrypt and encryption of stored API keys. However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information and are not liable for unauthorized access resulting from circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • AI providers — your prompts are sent to the provider you have configured, as described in Section 4
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Infrastructure — Upstash (database) and Vercel (hosting) necessarily process data as part of providing the Service

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your account data is retained in Redis for as long as your account exists. Session tokens expire automatically after 30 days of inactivity.

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at braxtonvogel.com. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Local data (chat history, vault files) is stored only on your device and can be deleted by you at any time by removing the relevant files from your installation directory.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at braxtonvogel.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
